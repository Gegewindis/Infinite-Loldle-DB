Regions
'Bandle City','A whimsical land of yordles and curious explorers full of mischief and discovery.'
'Bilgewater','A lawless pirate port of cutthroats and adventurers seeking fortune on the high seas.'
'Demacia','A proud and disciplined kingdom that values honor, duty and justice above all else.'
'Freljord','A harsh, frozen region where rival tribes battle for survival and ancient powers awaken.'
'Ionia','Mystical lands of harmony and spiritual magic, scarred by war and inner conflict.'
'Ixtal','A secretive jungle nation mastering elemental magic and keeping outsiders at bay.'
'Noxus','A brutal expansionist empire where strength, ambition and ruthlessness rule.'
'Piltover','The City of Progress, driven by innovation, invention and hextech advancement.'
'Zaun','An undercity of unchecked chem-tech industry, thriving in pollution and invention.'
'Shadow Isles','Once blessed isles now shrouded in Black Mist, haunted by spirits and undeath.'
'Shurima','An ancient desert empire risen from ruin, echoing with myths of past glory.'
'Targon','Towering mountains where mortals seek celestial power among the stars.'
'The Void','A cosmic abyss of monstrous beings seeking to devour Runeterra itself.'
'Runeterra','The wider world beyond specific regions, home to wanderers and untied souls.'
