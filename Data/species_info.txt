Species
'Human','Ordinary humans from Runeterra with no inherent magical biology, though many wield magic or technology.'
'Vastaya','Magical humanoid beings descended from ancient spirits, often with animal traits.'
'Yordle','Small, magical beings from Bandle City with long lifespans and whimsical appearances.'
'Undead','Beings who have died but persist through necromancy, curses, or dark magic.'
'Darkin','Corrupted god-warriors bound to weapons, sustained by blood magic.'
'Voidborn','Creatures spawned or transformed by the Void, driven by consumption and destruction.'
'Brackern','Ancient crystalline scorpion-like beings native to Shurima.'
'Iceborn','Humans altered by true ice, granting supernatural resilience and strength.'
'Troll','Large, regenerative humanoids native to the Freljord.'
'Spirit','Beings originating from the Spirit Realm, often embodying emotions or concepts.'
'Demon','Malevolent entities that feed on mortal emotions such as fear, pain, or secrets.'
'Dragon','Powerful elemental draconic beings tied to magic and domination.'
'Construct','Artificial beings created through magic or technology.'
'Ascended','God-like beings created by the Sun Disc of Shurima.'
'Celestial','Cosmic entities originating beyond Runeterra, associated with stars and creation.'
'Aspect','Mortals chosen or possessed by Celestial beings to act as their avatars.'
'Revenant','Individuals resurrected through vengeance or cursed will rather than necromancy.'
'Yeti', 'The Yeti were ancient magical mammals of the Freljord. Intelligent and peaceful, they alone could forge True Ice and lived in harmony with creatures like the Yordles.'
